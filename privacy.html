Privacy Policy - CineMatch

Effective Date: February 23, 2026
Developer: Chase Metoyer
Contact: support@cinematch.support

1. Introduction

CineMatch ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using CineMatch, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username/display name
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Authentication information from Sign in with Apple or other providers

User Content and Messaging

To operate matching and messaging, we may collect:

  • Movie preferences and match selections
  • Theater preferences
  • Messages and content shared in chats
  • Match history
  • Reactions and in-app interactions
  • Activity timestamps

Usage and Diagnostics

We may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, performance data, and push notification tokens. This helps us maintain stability and improve performance.

Location (if you allow it)

If you grant permission, we may use your location to suggest nearby theaters and showtimes. You can revoke location access in your device settings.

3. Tracking, Analytics, and App Tracking Transparency

We may use analytics and identifiers to help measure performance and improve the app. If any collected data is used to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, we will request permission via Apple AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) before collecting that data. If you do not grant permission, we will not use identifiers that require tracking consent.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Face Data

Profile photos may contain face data. We store profile photos as part of your account profile. We do not store facial geometry or perform facial recognition.

If we perform face detection to help prevent spam or validate that a profile photo contains a human face, this processing is done temporarily and only to complete that validation. We do not store any derived face geometry or share it with third parties.

5. How We Share Information

We use trusted service providers who process data on our behalf, such as Supabase (database/back-end infrastructure), authentication providers, push notification providers, and analytics providers. Information is shared only as needed to provide the service and as described in this policy.

We do not share biometric templates or face geometry with any third parties.

6. User-Generated Content Safety

  • CineMatch allows user-generated content (profiles, photos, and messages).
  • We have a zero-tolerance approach to objectionable content and harmful behavior.
  • Users can report and block other users. We review and act on reports in a timely manner, including removing content and/or suspending accounts that violate our Terms or community standards.

7. Data Retention

We retain account and interaction data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or withdraw consent for your personal data.

9. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Age Requirement

CineMatch is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact support@cinematch.support.
